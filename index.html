ABOUT COLLEGE

GHOUSIA COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

Ghousia College of Engineering is an engineering college located in Ramanagaram in the southern Indian state of Karnataka. It is managed by the Ghousia Industrial and Engineering Trust, Bangalore. Ghousia Industrial & Engineering Trust, was started during the year 1962. Wikipedia Address: B.M Road, Vijaya Nagar, Ramanagara, Karnataka 562159 Phone: 080 2727 1353
